About Us

Mishricky Medical is located in central Wagga Wagga at the Wagga Wagga Specialist Medical Centre. We provide a range of quality general practice services and are committed to delivering compassionate, evidence-based care.

We are a teaching practice and regularly host medical students. We are fully accredited through AGPAL, eHealth registered, and have easy claim facilities onsite.
